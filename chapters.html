What your chapter gets

You're not starting from scratch

A finalized curriculum

Meeting-by-meeting slide decks, worksheets, and resources — objectives set for each period so leaders can focus on running the club, not inventing it.

Shared guest speakers

Chapters across the network share access to guest speakers — researchers, professors, and college students — scheduled through the year.

Peer editing across schools

Your members swap drafts with students at other chapters for feedback beyond your own club room.

A path to publication

Finished papers are eligible for the {{ brand }} Journal, plus guidance on competitions and journals organized by deadline and topic.
